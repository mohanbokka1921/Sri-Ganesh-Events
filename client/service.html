Our Services

Sri Ganesh Events provides professional decoration and event management services for all types of celebrations and special occasions. Our experienced team ensures every event is beautifully organized and memorable.

Wedding Decorations

We create elegant wedding decorations including stages, mandap setups, floral arches, entrance decorations, photo booths, and complete venue styling.

Reception Decorations

Beautiful reception stage decorations with premium flowers, lighting effects, backdrops and customized themes.

Haldi Ceremony Decorations

Traditional and modern haldi decorations featuring yellow flower arrangements, floral swings, umbrellas and cultural setups.

Customized birthday party decorations including balloon arches, flower arrangements, cake tables and themed backdrops.
